Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

To deliver your invitation we store: your contact details (phone/email), wedding details you submit (names, date, venue, photos), your payment slip screenshot, your guest list, and guests' RSVPs and wishes.

How it's used

Only to build and operate your invitation, verify payment, and contact you about your order. We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

Where it lives

Data is stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Payment slips are kept only until verification plus a short accounting period, then deleted on request.

Guest data

Guest names and RSVPs are visible to the couple on their private dashboard. Wishes submitted by guests appear publicly on the invitation page.

Removal

Email sovathapann@gmail.com to have your invitation and all associated data deleted at any time.
